What do I do?

how I can help you:

I work with each client and help support them with bespoke therapy designed specifically to fit their unique needs. People don’t always know the root of their struggles. I help discover what this is and create a path to change it. You have the power to change your life today. Through weekly or bi-weekly sessions, we explore the relationships in your life and help transform them to being happier and healthier.

Here's how it works.

My formula for creating positive change:
01

Reflect

The first step is sharing where you’re coming from and considering where you want to be. It is most important that you decide to make a change.

02

Discover

Together we can dive deep, exploring what might be happening, noticing any patterns in your life, and looking at what is holding you back.

03

Build

Once we uncover what’s blocking you in your life, we will embark on a journey of your learning and growth, as I support you towards making positive changes.

04

Maintain

Building positive habits and creating changes can take time and accountability. Even once things start feeling better, having support to keep it that way is key.

You can do it

When you want to make a change, anything is possible. You know your own life, and I know the science, theory, and application. Together we can work towards building whatever it is you want to create and make your dreams come true.

Dating, Family, Health, and Happiness Coach

Miriam Goldberg

Based in Los Angeles with my husband and dog, living a vegan lifestyle, traveling the world, and making music and art. After growing up here, I spent the past 11 years living in New York, Israel, Germany, and London. I've always had an interest in the relationships people have with others, and themselves, and have always helped the people around me grow and find their true happiness. I have a Graduate Certificate from Harvard in Topics in Human Behavior. Currently, I'm in the process of getting my Master's in Psychology at Harvard, where I've studied in depth, everything from The Psychology of Close Relationships, to The Science of Physical Activity Applied to Health and Wellbeing. My greatest passion is helping people achieve unlimited happiness in their relationships. Let's work together to achieve whatever it is you are looking for.
